Welcome to LumiFlix. Your privacy is important to us, and this Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our website and services.

By accessing or using LumiFlix, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

LumiFlix is designed to provide a smooth and secure streaming experience. We may collect limited information to improve our services, including:

  • Basic device information (browser type, device type, screen size)
  • Usage data (pages visited, interactions, playback activity)
  • Anonymous technical data for performance and analytics

We do not require account registration to browse or stream content unless explicitly stated for specific features.

2. How We Use Information

The information we collect is used strictly to:

  • Improve website performance and stability
  • Optimize streaming quality and compatibility
  • Enhance user experience across devices
  • Analyze traffic and feature usage
We do not sell, rent, or trade user data to third parties.

3. Cookies & Local Storage

LumiFlix may use cookies or local storage technologies to:

  • Remember user preferences
  • Enable features such as wishlist or continue watching
  • Improve loading speed and responsiveness

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time.

LumiFlix may display content metadata, ratings, or trailers sourced from third-party services.

Please note:

  • Third-party services may have their own privacy policies
  • LumiFlix is not responsible for external websites or services linked from our platform
  • We encourage users to review the privacy policies of any third-party services they interact with

5. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect our platform against:

  • Unauthorized access
  • Data misuse
  • Loss or alteration of information

However, no internet-based system can be guaranteed 100% secure.

6. Children's Privacy

LumiFlix is not intended for children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe such information has been collected, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect:

  • Service improvements
  • Legal or regulatory requirements
  • Platform feature updates

Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our Contact Us page.
